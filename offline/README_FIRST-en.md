# LJT-S Offline Package (2026-07-10)

This package is designed for administering LJT-S without relying on an internet connection.
It is distributed separately from the live GitHub Pages site and does not change the public URL.

## Before administration

1. Extract the Zip file. Do not open `START_HERE.html` from inside the Zip archive.
2. Keep `START_HERE.html` and the `audio` folder together in their extracted folder.
3. Connect headphones or earphones.
4. A current version of Chrome or Edge is recommended.

## Participant procedure

1. Double-click `START_HERE.html`.
2. Enter the anonymous ID and any other information provided by the researcher.
3. Read the instructions and participation notice, then complete the sound check.
4. Complete 4 practice trials and 40 main-test trials.
5. Save the CSV from the completion screen.
6. Submit the saved CSV according to the researcher's instructions.

## Important

- Email submission is disabled in this offline package. Results are not sent automatically.
- The downloaded CSV is the participant-side authoritative result file. Save it before closing the browser.
- The `audio` folder contains exactly 44 files: 40 main-test files and 4 practice files. Do not move, rename, or delete them.
- Incomplete responses and completed results may be stored in the browser for up to 24 hours for resume or repeat download. Use “Clear saved data on this device” on the result screen, and follow the researcher's data-handling procedure on shared computers.
- If a participant enters a real name, the CSV contains personal information. Store it according to the approved research protocol.

## Troubleshooting

- If audio does not play, confirm that the Zip was extracted and that the `audio` folder remains beside `START_HERE.html`.
- Check the browser volume, the operating system's audio output, and the headphone connection.
- Do not reload the page during a session or move `START_HERE.html` away from its package folder.
